# Recommendation Framework

Use a stable recommendation vocabulary.

## Labels
- `Priority 1`: strong demand, clear buyer logic, plausible route, acceptable proof burden.
- `Priority 2`: attractive but with a meaningful dependency or narrower fit.
- `Selective`: pursue only for specific products, pathways, buyers, or producer profiles.
- `Monitor`: not a near-term priority, but worth tracking.
- `No-go`: do not pursue under current conditions.

## Every recommendation should include
- Why now
- Why demand exists
- Buyer / counterparty logic
- Why the producer or operating base can compete
- Gating requirements
- Better alternatives / competing routes
- Risks and no-go conditions
- What would change this conclusion
- Evidence confidence
