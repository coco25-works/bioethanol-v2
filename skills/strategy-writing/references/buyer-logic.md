# Buyer Logic

Do not write strategy as if “the market” buys. Name the actual buyer class.

## Common buyer classes
- obligated fuel suppliers
- refiners and blenders
- traders and aggregators
- airlines or aviation intermediaries
- shipping-linked buyers
- compliance-value intermediaries

## Questions to answer
- Who captures compliance value?
- Who would sign an offtake or supply agreement?
- Who bears proof and certification burden?
- Does buyer concentration strengthen or weaken the opportunity?
