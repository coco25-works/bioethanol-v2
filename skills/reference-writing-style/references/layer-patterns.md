# Layer Patterns

Different layers need different writing behavior.

## Source register
- Be factual and restrained.
- Explain relevance briefly.
- Surface uncertainty and gaps explicitly.

## Country reference
- Explain how the market works.
- Make buyer pull and access conditions concrete.
- Use worked examples where market mechanics are not obvious.

## Pathway reference
- Explain what is in and out of scope.
- Compare route treatment across markets.
- Clarify proof burden and route attractiveness.

## Market analysis
- Explain the comparison dimensions first.
- State the finding directly.
- Include disqualifiers, not just positives.

## Strategy
- Make the recommendation explicit.
- Name buyers, timing, gating requirements, better alternatives, and no-go conditions.
- Keep the recommendation evidence-backed and non-promotional.
