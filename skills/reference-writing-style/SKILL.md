# Reference Writing Style

Use this skill to control prose quality across the full repository. It governs how the writing should read, not what the strategy recommendation should be.

## Core Rules
- Open with why the reader should care, not a dry definition.
- Put diagrams before the structured data they explain.
- Put prose between complex tables.
- State the pattern the table reveals after the table.
- Write differently by layer: source, reference, analysis, and strategy do not sound the same.

## Layer Awareness
- For source files, prioritize authority, metadata clarity, and caveats.
- For entity and pathway references, explain mechanics and practical meaning.
- For market analysis, explain comparisons and disqualifiers.
- For strategy, explain recommendations, buyer logic, timing, gating, and stop conditions.

## Output Expectations
- Make dense content readable without dumbing it down.
- Make the prose usable for scanning and for close reading.
- Keep the tone authoritative, direct, and non-bureaucratic.
