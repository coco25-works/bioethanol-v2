# Novel Feedstock Developer Strategy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Strategy and BD teams developing new feedstock or conversion routes  
**Decision supported:** How novel-feedstock players should position themselves

## Executive Takeaway
Novel-feedstock developers should position around **future premium differentiation**, not near-term commodity scale.

## Best Strategic Logic
- focus on routes that can become advanced or premium-treated
- avoid competing head-on with conventional incumbent supply
- treat this as a build-first strategy, not a fast-volume strategy
