# Gas and Effluent Operator Strategy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Strategy and BD teams operating gas / effluent assets  
**Decision supported:** How gas-side operators should think about export versus local monetization

## Executive Takeaway
Gas and effluent operators have strategic relevance, but the export route is harder than for liquid-fuel pathways.

## Best Strategic Logic
- prioritize domestic or regional monetization first where possible
- treat maritime or export-linked gas routes as build-first opportunities
- do not outrank waste-based liquid-fuel routes in near-term planning
