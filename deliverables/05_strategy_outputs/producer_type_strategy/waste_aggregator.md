# Waste Aggregator Strategy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Strategy and BD teams in collection / aggregation businesses  
**Decision supported:** How waste aggregators should position themselves in the value chain

## Executive Takeaway
Waste aggregators sit closer to the real value switch than many producers do.

Why:
- the premium depends on waste proof,
- and waste aggregators often control the earliest and most fragile part of that proof chain.

## Best Strategic Logic
- invest in collection quality and documentary integrity
- use proof discipline as a competitive advantage
- build around routes where strong waste classification directly unlocks premium value
