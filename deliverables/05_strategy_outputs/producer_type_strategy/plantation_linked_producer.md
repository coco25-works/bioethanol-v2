# Plantation-Linked Producer Strategy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Strategy and BD teams in plantation-linked businesses  
**Decision supported:** How plantation-linked producers should position themselves

## Executive Takeaway
Plantation-linked producers should build around **waste-side value**, not around conventional palm-fuel exports into Europe.

## Best Strategic Logic
- prioritize `POME` and residue-based routes where defensible
- avoid using conventional palm-based European liquid-fuel exports as the main thesis

## What This Means
The strongest plantation-linked opportunity is often not the main crop itself. It is the waste and residue system around it.
