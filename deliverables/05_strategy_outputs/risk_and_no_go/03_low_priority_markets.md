# Monitor Markets

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD and leadership teams  
**Decision supported:** Which markets should be monitored rather than actively pursued first

## Executive Takeaway
Monitor does not mean irrelevant. It means the market is not the best use of scarce execution capacity right now.

## Current Monitor Markets
- Japan as a first-wave destination
- China as a current entry market
- Brazil, Indonesia, Malaysia, and Thailand as demand markets rather than benchmark / context markets
- California as a first-wave Southeast Asia route for most cases

## What This Means For Our Team
- monitor these markets intelligently
- do not let them distract from the strongest Europe-first routes
