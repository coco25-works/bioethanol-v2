# No-Go Conditions

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD, compliance, and leadership teams  
**Decision supported:** Which routes should be rejected outright under current assumptions

## Executive Takeaway
Some routes are not just weaker. They are poor strategic uses of time.

## No-Go Conditions
- conventional palm-based liquid-fuel exports into Europe as a flagship route
- conventional ethanol as the main export-growth thesis
- any premium route without defensible waste proof
- any serious compliance-market route without credible certification / approval capability

## What This Means For Our Team
- use these conditions as hard stop rules during screening
- do not spend senior management time re-arguing structurally weak routes
