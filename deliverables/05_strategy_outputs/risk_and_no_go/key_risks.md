# Key Risks

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Leadership, BD, finance, and compliance teams  
**Decision supported:** Which risks could materially weaken or collapse the strategy

## Executive Takeaway
The biggest risks are not abstract. They sit in:
- feedstock classification,
- documentation quality,
- regulatory change,
- and value-capture leakage.

## Most Important Risks
- waste classification tightening
- proof-chain failure
- premium-value volatility
- regulatory shifts in key Europe-first markets
- over-reliance on one market or one route

## What This Means For Our Team
- treat documentation as strategic risk control
- diversify routes where practical
- keep the update and watchlist process active
