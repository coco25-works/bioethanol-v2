# Offtake Model Options

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD and finance teams  
**Decision supported:** Which offtake structures best preserve producer value capture

## Executive Takeaway
The contract structure can decide whether a good route becomes a good business.

## Best Commercial Logic
- prefer structures that preserve some link to compliance-value upside
- avoid overly simple contracts that give away premium value to the intermediary

## What This Means For Our Team
- negotiate for value capture, not just volume placement
- treat offtake structure as part of strategy, not just legal paperwork
