# Target Counterparties

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD and leadership teams  
**Decision supported:** Which buyer and intermediary types matter most in the target markets

## Executive Takeaway
The most important counterparties are not just “buyers.” They are the parties that can actually monetize compliance value.

## Priority Counterparty Types
- obligated fuel suppliers in Germany and the Netherlands
- importers and traders with compliance-market access
- Rotterdam-linked commercial intermediaries
- selected UK counterparties as secondary route operators

## What This Means For Our Team
- prioritize counterparties who can share premium value, not just lift physical volume
- distinguish between a logistics counterparty and a true monetization counterparty
