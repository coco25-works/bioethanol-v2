# Certification Requirements

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD, compliance, and operations teams  
**Decision supported:** What certification and proof capabilities are mandatory before active market pursuit

## Executive Takeaway
Certification is not a nice-to-have. It is market-access infrastructure.

## Core Requirement Logic
- Europe-first routes require strong sustainability certification and proof discipline
- US routes require route approval in addition to proof capability
- California requires route / CI treatment capability rather than only scheme-based proof

## What This Means For Our Team
- treat certification as a core execution workstream
- budget for proof and audit discipline early
- do not let BD pursue routes the proof system cannot support
