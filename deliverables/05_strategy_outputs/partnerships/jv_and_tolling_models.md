# JV and Tolling Models

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD and leadership teams  
**Decision supported:** When partnership structures add value and when they simply give value away

## Executive Takeaway
Use partnership structures when they unlock real access, not just because they sound strategically sophisticated.

## Best Use Cases
- JV where local market access or infrastructure is genuinely difficult
- tolling only where it solves a real asset or market-entry problem

## What This Means For Our Team
- default to the simplest structure that preserves value capture
- only add structure when it solves a real bottleneck
