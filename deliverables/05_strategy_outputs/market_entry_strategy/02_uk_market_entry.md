# UK Market Entry Strategy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD and strategy teams  
**Decision supported:** How the UK should be used in the market-entry sequence

## Executive Takeaway
Use the UK as a **secondary European route**, not the first route.

It is useful because it is commercially usable and operationally flexible in some cases, but it usually sits behind Germany and the Netherlands.

## Best Use Of The UK
- as a backup or diversification route
- as a route where proof-handling flexibility helps
- as a second-wave European market after core EU entry is underway

## What Must Be True First
- qualifying route for stronger UK treatment
- recognized certification
- buyer access that makes the UK route worth management attention
