# Singapore Hub Strategy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD, operations, and logistics teams  
**Decision supported:** What role Singapore should play in execution

## Executive Takeaway
Use Singapore as a **hub strategy**, not as the center of demand strategy.

Its value is in:
- logistics,
- trade coordination,
- price discovery,
- and maritime relevance.

## Best Use Of Singapore
- routing and trading support
- commercial coordination node
- potential maritime-linked support point

## What This Means For Our Team
- build Singapore into the operating model where helpful
- do not confuse hub value with destination-market value
