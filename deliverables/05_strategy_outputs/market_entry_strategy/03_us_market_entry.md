# US Market Entry Strategy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD, compliance, and leadership teams  
**Decision supported:** When the company should commit to US market entry and when it should defer

## Executive Takeaway
Treat the US as a **serious but second-wave market**.

It is worth pursuing only if the company is prepared to fund and manage the regulatory-access burden.

## Entry Logic
- pursue only with clear pathway-approval plan
- do not treat the US as a fast-entry market
- use Europe-first routes to build value and experience first unless the company already has US access capability

## What Must Be True First
- willingness to fund approval and compliance work
- clarity on likely route category and economics
- buyer / importer structure that does not absorb all upside

## Better Alternative
If the company needs faster execution and lower access burden, Europe remains the better first move.
