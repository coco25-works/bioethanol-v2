# Japan Market Entry Strategy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD and strategy teams  
**Decision supported:** How Japan should be treated in the entry sequence

## Executive Takeaway
Treat Japan as a **niche or relationship-driven market**, not a first-wave export destination.

## Best Use Of Japan
- targeted relationship-led opportunities
- selective bio-ETBE-linked routes
- strategic watch market for future policy change

## What Must Be True First
- strong counterparties or trading-house access
- route economics that work without a major credit premium
- product fit with Japanese market logic
