# Biomethanol Strategy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD, strategy, and leadership teams  
**Decision supported:** Whether biomethanol should be pursued now or monitored as an emerging route

## Executive Takeaway
**Monitor**, and build understanding rather than immediate large-scale commercial execution.

Biomethanol is strategically interesting because shipping is moving, but it is still not a first-wave commercial route compared with HVO / biodiesel.

## Recommendation
- **Monitor** with build-first preparation

## Why Now
- maritime demand is becoming more serious
- methanol-fuel logic is becoming more relevant in shipping
- but demand formation is still uneven

## What Must Be True First
- clearer buyer pull in maritime channels
- viable bunkering / logistics route
- credible route economics beyond theory

## Better Alternative
Until biomethanol matures, the company should prioritize waste-based liquid-fuel routes with clearer monetization.

## What Would Make Us Stop
- if the route remains infrastructure-heavy without corresponding buyer pull
- if maritime adoption slows materially
