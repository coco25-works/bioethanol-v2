## Why This Market Matters
Thailand has biofuel blending mandates for both ethanol and biodiesel and is a significant regional biofuel producer. Thailand's sugarcane and cassava-based ethanol production is the largest in SE Asia. For biodiesel, B7 remains the standard reference point and B20 is recognized in policy tracking; B10 sales were discontinued in 2024. Understanding Thai policy matters for regional feedstock dynamics and assessing whether Thailand is a competitor, market destination, or supply source.

### What this means for non-specialists
Thailand is a competitor and benchmark market. Partial English sources are available from EPPO.
