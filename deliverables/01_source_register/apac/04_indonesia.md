## Why This Market Matters
Indonesia is the world's largest palm oil producer and operates one of the most aggressive domestic biodiesel mandates globally. The B40 mandate, implemented from 2025, absorbs significant domestic palm oil production, directly affecting global palm oil availability and pricing. For SE Asia-based producers sourcing palm-derived feedstocks (POME, PFAD, palm residues), understanding Indonesian domestic demand and export policy is essential.

### What this means for non-specialists
Indonesia is a supply-context and competitor market. The sources below show the domestic biodiesel mandate structure. Indonesian-language sources are flagged; the mandate is well-documented internationally.
