# China Source Register

**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD team assessing Chinese biofuel context
**Decision supported:** What is the regulatory basis for bio-based fuels in China and what demand signals exist
**Coverage scope:** Chinese fuel policy, ethanol blending mandates, and relevant energy regulations
**Baseline date:** 2026-04-21
**Wave:** 2

## Why This Market Matters
China has the world's largest potential biofuel demand by population and fuel consumption. China's ethanol blending policy has gone through multiple phases — from an ambitious E10 mandate (2017) that was effectively paused, to more recent signals of renewed support. Even small policy shifts in China can affect global feedstock flows and pricing due to the sheer scale of the market.

### What this means for non-specialists
China matters for scale potential but the evidence base is weaker than the European and US layers. The key concern is that all primary Chinese sources are in Chinese; translation gaps are flagged as high-priority.
