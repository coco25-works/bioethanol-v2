# Malaysia Source Register

**Primary audience:** Commercial / BD team assessing Malaysian biofuel context
**Decision supported:** What is Malaysia's biodiesel policy and how does it affect feedstock supply for SE Asia producers
**Coverage scope:** Malaysian biodiesel mandates, palm oil policy, and relevant energy regulations
**Baseline date:** 2026-04-23
**Wave:** 2

## Why This Market Matters
Malaysia is the world's second-largest palm oil producer. Its biodiesel blending policy is best treated as a B10 baseline with B20 pilots or targeted use and a planned move toward higher blends, including B12/B15 rollout signals in 2026. Malaysian palm oil policy and biodiesel mandates directly affect global palm oil availability. For SE Asia producers using palm-derived feedstocks, understanding Malaysian domestic demand is essential.

### What this means for non-specialists
Malaysia is a supply-context and competitor market. English-language sources are more accessible here than in Indonesia.
