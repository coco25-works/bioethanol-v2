**Baseline date:** 2026-04-23
**Wave:** 1

## Why This Market Matters
The European Union is the largest regulated demand center for bio-based fuels globally. The Renewable Energy Directive framework creates binding targets, tradable compliance mechanisms, and pathway-specific treatment that directly pull compliant molecules into the market. RED III raised the 2030 transport sub-target and tightened Annex IX treatment. FuelEU Maritime creates a new demand signal for maritime biofuels starting 2025. Member-state implementation varies, which creates asymmetry opportunities across otherwise similar compliance regimes.

### What this means for non-specialists
The EU is the biggest market in this repository. The sources below prove that the demand is real and that the legal rules supporting the strategy are verifiable. If these sources hold up under review, the analysis built on them is trustworthy.
