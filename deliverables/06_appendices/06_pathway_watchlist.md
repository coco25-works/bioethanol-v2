# Pathway Watchlist

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21  
**Purpose:** Track which pathways need active monitoring for regulatory or market changes.

### Why This File Matters
The top-ranked pathways in this repository depend on specific regulatory treatment. If that treatment changes, the strategy can change. This file tells the team what to watch at the pathway level.

| Pathway | Watch item | Why it matters | Watch trigger | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Waste-based HVO/FAME | Annex IX Part A reclassification risk | Core pathway for Priority 1 strategy; reclassification would collapse premium | Commission delegated act proposals | Watching |
| Waste-based HVO/FAME | UCO fraud enforcement | Increasing fraud scrutiny could tighten verification requirements | Major enforcement actions in EU | Watching |
| Waste-based HVO/FAME | POME classification | POME waste classification may face scrutiny if volumes grow | Certification body guidance | Watching |
| Conventional biodiesel | Conventional cap changes | RED III already tightens caps; further tightening possible | RED III transposition | Watching |
| Advanced bioethanol | Lignocellulosic counting multiplier phase-out | Multipliers are time-limited by design | RED III implementation timeline | Watching |
| Bio-ETBE | Japan policy shift | Japan moving to direct E10 blending would change pathway requirements | METI policy announcements | Watching |
| Biomethanol | FuelEU Maritime uptake | Maritime demand depends on shipping industry adoption | Methanol-capable vessel orders; FuelEU compliance data | Watching |
| Bio-LNG | FuelEU Maritime + liquefaction | Maritime LNG demand and liquefaction infrastructure development | Singapore/EU bunkering data | Watching |
| SAF (HEFA) | ReFuelEU Aviation implementation | SAF demand from 2025; HEFA is the most relevant route for SE Asia | Fuel supplier compliance data | Watching |
