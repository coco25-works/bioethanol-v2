# Legal Citation Index

**Last updated:** 2026-04-23  
**Purpose:** Map of primary legal instruments by jurisdiction with links to source-register files and official URLs.

### Why This File Matters
This index lets the reader verify any legal reference quickly. If a country file or strategy file cites a regulation, this index shows where the authoritative text is and where the source register logged it.

## How To Use This Index
- Find a legal instrument by jurisdiction.
- Each entry links to the source-register file where it is logged and to the official URL.
- Use this when you need to verify a legal reference or find the authoritative text.

## Translation Notes
- German, Dutch, French, and most Japanese primary laws are in their original languages. Japan's Fuel Quality Control Act has an official English translation for the Act No. 69 of 2014 version, but later amendments and other Japanese sources still require Japanese-language review.
- EU legislation has official English texts on EUR-Lex.
- US legislation has official English texts on congress.gov and eCFR.
- UK legislation has official English texts on legislation.gov.uk.
